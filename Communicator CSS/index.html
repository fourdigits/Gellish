Network view

Display of the relations with directly related objects, their classifiers and possibly their values and units of measures.

Taxonomy view

Display of the supertype-subtype hierarchy and the classified individual things.

Product model view

Display of a product and its components and their aspects.

Documents view

Display of the documents that are related to the object in focus.

Activities view

Display of the activities, processes and events, their relations.and involved objects.
